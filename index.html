About

Hello! I’m Shakir Khan, a 25-year-old Legal Professional hailing from Tanodiya, Madhya Pradesh. I am currently working at the Agar Malwa District Court as a Legal Service Provider & Drafting Assistant. Alongside my professional drafting practice, I am actively pursuing my LLB from Vikram University, Ujjain, and am currently in my second semester.
Contact No. - +91 9171308643

My journey has been one of continuous learning, resilience, and evolution. Academically, I completed my B.Sc. (PCM) in 2021 and later pursued a PGDCA from SAM Global University, Bhopal. Early in my career, I spent nearly two years at Dynamic Beneficial Accord Marketing Pvt. Ltd., Ujjain, where I developed strong communication skills and a disciplined work ethic.

Driven by a hunger for knowledge, I spent the next three years preparing for government competitive exams. During this phase, I discovered a strong interest in technology. I dedicated two years to mastering frontend development (HTML, CSS, JS, React.js) and subsequently worked as a Frontend Developer at Letskillify. This technical phase taught me analytical thinking and extreme attention to detail.

However, the defining turning point in my career arrived in August 2025, when I stepped into the legal field. Joining the Agar Malwa District Court made me realize my true calling. Interestingly, the precision and focus I learned while writing code perfectly translated into crafting flawless Hindi legal drafts, affidavits, and official administrative applications.

Today, law is not just my profession; it is my ultimate passion and primary source of personal growth. My diverse background—from a science student to a frontend developer, and now a dedicated legal professional—has given me a highly adaptable and unique perspective. I am now fully committed to mastering court procedures and bridging the gap between my practical court experience and LLB studies, with the steadfast goal of becoming a successful and respected Advocate.
